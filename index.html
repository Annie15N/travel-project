Why Granada?

Granada is one of the most charming cities in Europe

Granada, Spain is quintessential on a visit to this part of Spain. Not only beautiful to the eye, but the city is also a treasure chest of surprises. Certainly most known for the Alhambra, the palace and fortress that sits in the center of the city, it’s a destination for travelers wanting to dive into history.

Amidst the Islamic architecture, you find winding streets that span up and down the hillsides of Granada. Within them, old-world bars serving typical tapas of the region and strong sweet wine, something you can’t miss while there. And of course, plenty of local shops selling goods that hail from the east.

TAPAS

My favourite tapas bars in Granada

La Telefónica

El restaurante La Telefónica es un verdadero tesoro culinario especializado en cocina andaluza. Con una atención meticulosa a los detalles, este lugar te cautivará con su selección de platos tradicionales. Restaurante familiar y acogedor, en el que trabajamos con el mejor producto. En La Telefónica, cada bocado es una celebración de los sabores auténticos de Andalucía, transportándote a la esencia de la cocina tradicional.

Address

C/ Arco De Las Orejas,1 | 18001 Granada

What I like about it

Good atmosphere, excellent tapas and typical local dishes.

Perromedio Taberna

Perromedio Taberna in Granada is a charming spot known for its cute decor with family photos, the owner preparing dishes in front of guests, and sweet tomatoes. The friendly and funny staff provide excellent service, suggest menu items and wines, and offer fantastic cheese boards. Guests praise the quality Iberian ham, delicious tapas like cheesecake without crust, and the cozy ambiance.

Address

C. Navas, 29, Centro | 18009 Granada

What I like about it

The freshly baked bread with olives and tomatoes.

Discover more

Bodegas Castañeda

Bodegas Castañeda no es solo un lugar para comer; es una experiencia completa que combina historia, cultura y gastronomía auténtica. Bodegas Castañeda is a classic, traditional tapas bar in Granada and famous for its wines. Son famosas sus tablas (frías y calientes), patés y productos ibéricos. Si buscas el típico ambiente granaino y buenas tapas a buen precio este es tu sitio, siempre pensando que habrá gente y mucho ambiente.

Address

Travesia Almireceros 1-3 | 18010 Granada

What I like about it

The jamon tapas board is insane!
